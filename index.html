About Us

Science & Engineering Solutions, LLC (SEScience) is a specialized consulting and research firm focused on advancing analytical science, environmental engineering, and applied research. With deep expertise in microplastics, nanoplastics, emerging contaminants, and advanced analytical methods, we support government agencies, utilities, research institutions, and private laboratories in solving complex scientific challenges.

Our mission is to bridge cutting-edge research with practical, defensible solutions that improve environmental protection, public health, and regulatory decision-making.

Leadership Team

Dr. Maryam Salehi

President

Dr. Salehi holds a Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Purdue University and is a recognized leader in microplastics and polymer research. Her work spans contaminant fate and transport, polymer characterization, wastewater and drinking water treatment, and method development for emerging pollutants.

Dr. Salehi has led multidisciplinary research programs, collaborated with federal and state agencies, and published extensively in peer-reviewed journals. Her leadership ensures scientific rigor, innovation, and strategic vision across all SEALLC projects.

Dr. Alexander Ccanccapa

Scientific Director

Dr. Ccanccapa is an environmental toxicologist and analytical scientist with extensive experience in mass spectrometry, microplastics analysis, pharmaceuticals, pesticides, and emerging contaminants across environmental and biological matrices.

He has developed and validated advanced LC-MS/MS, HRMS, Py-GC/MS, ICP-OES, and FTIR methods, and has led projects supporting regulatory compliance, risk assessment, and method standardization. His expertise bridges analytical chemistry, toxicology, and applied environmental science.
