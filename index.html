Advancing Microplastics and Nanoplastics Analysis

At Science & Engineering Solutions, LLC, we provide specialized technical, analytical, and training services focused on microplastics and nanoplastics in environmental and biological samples.

Our mission is to empower water and wastewater utilities, research groups, and analytical laboratories with the tools and expertise needed to accurately detect, quantify, and reduce microplastic pollution.

About Us

With more than a decade of academic research in microplastics, we witnessed firsthand the growing challenges faced by industry and water and wastewater utilities in addressing microplastic pollution and its impacts on water and food safety.

Our company was founded to bridge the gap between cutting-edge scientific research and practical, defensible analytical tools needed for regulatory readiness and environmental protection.

Our values are rooted in scientific integrity, innovation, collaboration, and service—delivering evidence-based solutions for a cleaner, safer future.
