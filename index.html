About Us

Science & Engineering Solutions, LLC (SEALLC) provides specialized scientific, engineering, and research consulting services focused on microplastics, nanoplastics, environmental contaminants, and advanced analytical challenges.

We support environmental laboratories, water and wastewater utilities, academic institutions, and industry partners with technically rigorous, data-driven solutions.

Leadership Team

Our leadership combines extensive experience in environmental chemistry, toxicology, mass spectrometry, and regulatory science.

The team has led method development, validation, and consulting efforts across environmental, biological, and medical-device-related applications.
