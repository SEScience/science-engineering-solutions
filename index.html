About Us

Science & Engineering Solutions, LLC (SEALLC) provides specialized scientific, engineering, and research consulting services to industry, academia, and public-sector clients. We focus on delivering data-driven, technically rigorous solutions to complex environmental and analytical challenges.

Our expertise spans microplastics and nanoplastics analysis, advanced analytical method development, treatment optimization, laboratory training, and toxicology consulting for environmental, biological, and medical-device related applications.
